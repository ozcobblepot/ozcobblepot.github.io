Frequently Asked Questions

1. What types of bags does Suede Bag offer?

Suede Bag specializes in high-quality leather bags designed for durability, elegance, and functionality. Our collection includes tote bags, backpacks, handbags, wallets, and travel accessories. Each piece is handcrafted to combine timeless design with modern practicality.

2. Where are Suede Bag products made?

All our products are proudly handcrafted in the Philippines by skilled artisans who use ethically sourced materials. We emphasize sustainability and quality in every step of production.

3. What makes Suede Bag unique?

At Suede Bag, we blend traditional craftsmanship with modern design to create leather bags that are not only stylish but also built to last. We prioritize ethical practices, use premium materials, and offer customizable options to suit your personal style.

4. How do I care for my suede or leather bag?

To ensure your bag stays in excellent condition:

a) Use a soft, dry cloth to wipe off dust and dirt.
b) For suede, use a suede brush to maintain its texture and avoid exposure to moisture.
c) Keep leather bags hydrated with a high-quality leather conditioner.
d) Store your bag in a dust bag when not in use to protect it from scratches and fading.

5. Are your products eco-friendly?

Yes, we are committed to sustainability. We source our leather responsibly and minimize waste in production. Additionally, we aim to create products that stand the test of time, reducing the need for frequent replacements.

6. Can I customize a Suede Bag product?

Absolutely! We offer personalization options such as monogramming or custom designs for select products. Please contact our team to discuss your customization ideas, and we’ll work with you to create a unique piece.

7. Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship our products worldwide. Shipping rates and delivery times may vary depending on your location. You can check shipping costs during the checkout process or contact us for more details.

8. What is your return and exchange policy?

We accept returns and exchanges within 30 days of purchase, provided the item is unused and in its original packaging. Personalized items, however, are non-refundable. For assistance with returns or exchanges, please contact our customer support team.

9. How can I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to check the status of your delivery through our shipping partner’s website.

10. Does Suede Bag offer warranty coverage?

Yes, we provide a 1-year warranty on all our products to cover any manufacturing defects. If you encounter any issues, please contact us with proof of purchase, and we’ll be happy to assist.

11. Do you offer bulk or corporate orders?

Yes, we accommodate bulk orders for events, giveaways, or corporate gifting. Discounts and customization options are available for larger orders. Contact us for inquiries and pricing details.

12. How can I contact Suede Bag?

You can reach us via email at support@suedebag.ph, or send us a message through our website’s contact form. We’re also active on social media—feel free to DM us on Instagram or Facebook.

13. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for online purchases. For in-store transactions, we accept cash and select digital payment options.

14. Does Suede Bag have a physical store?

Currently, we operate primarily online. However, we do host pop-up shops and participate in trade fairs. Follow us on social media to stay updated on our events.

15. What are your company values?

Suede Bag is dedicated to sustainability, quality, and customer satisfaction. We believe in creating products that celebrate Filipino craftsmanship and provide our customers with timeless pieces they can cherish for years.
